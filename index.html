The Critical Problem We're Solving

Trust Deficit

Current carbon markets suffer from double-counting, fraud, and lack of transparency, undermining environmental and financial value.

Scale Problem

Macro-level policies fail to leverage the decentralized power of billions of human actions needed for real impact.

Incentive Misalignment

There's no direct financial reward for sustainable choices, making it a moral decision rather than an economic one.

The GCIIP Solution

A five-layer architecture designed for trust, scale, and impact

1

Human Interface

Simple mobile app and USSD access for everyone to report Green Acts and track earnings.

2

AI Verification

Multi-modal AI automatically verifies environmental actions using satellite, IoT, and user data.

3

Carbon Integrity Ledger

Blockchain technology ensures every VGA is unique, traceable, and fraud-proof.

4

Economic Engine

Marketplace connecting VGA sellers (individuals/communities) with buyers (corporations).

5

GCIIP Foundation

Consortium-led governance ensuring fairness, transparency, and continuous improvement.
